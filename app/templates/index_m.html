    Brief Introduction

       Simao Pine Caterpillars, also known as brown pine caterpillars, belong to the genus Dendrolimus of Lepidoptera. They were first found in Simao, Yunnan Province, and named China. They are mainly distributed in Yunnan, Guizhou, Sichuan, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Anhui, Jiangxi, Fujian, Taiwan, Hainan and other provinces. Simao Pine Caterpillars mainly feed on Simao Pine, Yunnan pine, Huashan pine and other pine plants, and in the cloud Simao Pine is mainly damaged in the South Simao Area, but in the surrounding area of Kunming, it is mainly damaged by Dianya. Simao Pine Caterpillars are one of the six kinds of pine caterpillars that are most seriously damaged. When it is serious, it will make a large number of pine plants wither and die, like a fire, which will seriously affect the ecological environment and tourism in the relevant areas, and bring great inconvenience to the local people's production and life. Due to different geographical locations, Simao Pine Caterpillars in China The distribution in the south is special. Therefore, there are few studies on Dendrolimus punctatus abroad, and the domestic research results are quite fruitful. The observation of the morphological characteristics and living habits of Dendrolimus punctatus in each life stage provides a theoretical basis for the control of Dendrolimus punctatus
